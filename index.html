WHAT IS ATAL TINKERING LAB?

Atal tinkering lab is the flagship initiative of Atal Innovation Mission (AIM), Government of India to cultivate an innovative mindset amongst high school students across the country.

what is the purpose of ATL lab?

The objective of this scheme is to foster curiosity, creativity and imagination in young minds and inculcate skills such as design mind - set, computational thinking, adaptive learning, physical computing, rapid calculations,, mesurements etc


WHAT IS ATAL INNOVATION MISSION?
The Atal Innovation Mission (AIM) is a flagship initiative set up the NITI Aayog to promote innovation and entrepreneurship across the length and breadth of the country.

How many ATAL TINKERING LABS ARE THRE IN INDIA?

Out of 1000 ATAL TINKERING LABORATORIES, 187 will be established by the end of the financial year 2021-2022. out of 187 ATLs, 31 are being established across government schools of J&k while 50 will be established in several educational institutions like KVs,JNVs and private schools.IN report on 13-Dec-2021.Totally there are more than 8,700 ATAL TINKERING LABS IN INDIA>

WHAT IS NEW INDIA CHALLENGE?

Atal New India Challenge is an initiative by ATAL Innovation by Atal Innovation Mission aimed at supporting innovators to create products/solutions based on advance technologies in areas of national importance and social relevance through a grant-basd mechanism.

IN WHICH YEAR ATAL TINKERING LAB WAS STARTED?

2016

The Atal Tinkering Lab handbook is an attempt to capture the three key elements of the tinkering movement in India, that was launched in 2016.

WHY ARE ATL SKILLS IMPORTANT?

ATL skills help students prepare for, and demonstrate learning through, meaniingful assesment. They provide a common language that students and teachers can use to reflect on and articulate on the process of learning.

WHAT ARE SOME CRITICAL THINKING SKILLS?

The key of critical thinking skills are: analysis, interpretation, inference, explanation, self-regulaton, open-mindedness, and problem-solving.
